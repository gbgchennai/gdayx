Welcome to GDayX Chennai

GDayX Chennai is a daylong event with two sessions proceedings under one roof. The first set (Business Events) of proceeding dedicated for entrepreneurs, focused on ‘Using Digital & Web Technologies For Business Success’. The second (Developer Events) conducted for engineers, to gain in-depth knowledge through hands-on sessions, which will also include code labs sworn to cutting-edge web and mobile technologies.

Whether you’re hoping to socialize with fellow developers or entrepreneurs or to learn more about technologies for business success or aiming to develop your own products using Google’s APIs. GDayX is the perfect place to get started.
